BABYGENERATOR PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application BabyGenrator for mobile devices that was created by DBAI.  

Welcome to BabyGenrator’s Privacy Policy!

If you decide not to read this entire Privacy Policy, we want you to walk away with a few key points about BabyGenrator’s privacy practices:

  • Our application uses the newest technology of artificial intelligence to analyze two given faces and produce an image of your cute baby.
  • Due to the limited processing resources available on mobile devices, we use a cloud server to process photographs.
  • The App only uploads to the cloud the photographs that you specifically select for predicting.
    Photographs are temporarily cached on the cloud servers during the predicting process.
  • Photographs remain in the cloud for a limited period of 24-48 hours after you have last predicted the photograph, so that you can return to the image and make additional predicts if you so choose.
    All your photos will be deleted for up to 48 hours.
  • We do not use the photographs you provide when you use the App for any reason other than to provide you with the baby predicting functionality of the App.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please email datnguyenvan844@gmail.com.
